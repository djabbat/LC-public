Annals of Rejuvenation Science

Annals of Rejuvenation Science is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal dedicated to empirical and translational research on aging interventions, biomarker development, and preclinical strategies for healthspan extension. We publish rigorously validated studies that move longevity science from theory to measurable biological outcome.

The journal accepts intervention studies, biomarker validation work, pre-registered replications, negative results with adequate statistical power, and meta-analyses in the domain of aging and longevity. Open to preclinical and clinical research.
